Tourist places in India



1. GOA

Goa is a famous tourist destination in India. It is the smallest state in India. It is famous for its endless beaches, diverse seafood, fantastic nightlife, and stunning architecture. Goa is one of the few places in the world that opens 24X7. Goa is a combination of Indian and Portuguese cultures that is the most distinctive feature.The Goans are very friendly to visitors and also organize numerous festivals across the year. Goa's most well-known festivals are Goa Carnival and New Year. The seafood in Goa is fantastic. In India, Goa provides the best nightlife scenes with fashionable pubs, discotheques, beach shacks, a variety of clubs, and beautiful cafes.

2. Udaipur

It's one of Rajasthan's most popular tourist attractions. Udaipur is commonly called the City of Lakes. Udaipur is famous for azure lakes, majestic palaces, colorful culture, and wonderful cuisine. Along with a must-see site, this place is also the best place in India to enjoy luxury. One of the most amazing views and pleasures of the Udaipur tour is boating in the glistening lake Pichola. Udaipur is also called the "Venice of the East," it is undoubtedly one of India's most romantic destinations.

3.Jammu And Kashmir

Khardung La Pass, Gulmarg, Vaishno Devi, Dal Lake, Kashmir Valley, Sonmarg, Shalimar Bagh, Amarnath, Srinagar, Yusmarg, Betaab Valley, Patnitop, Sanasar, Gurez Valley, Pahalgam, Pulwama, and Chandanwari. The famous dishes of Kashmir that tourists must try are rogan josh, Kashmiri pulao, paneer Chaman, rajma, malai kofta, Kashmiri saag, aloo dum, gushtaba, yogurt lamb curry, and patisa. The Kahwa is the most famous drink in Kashmir.

4. Nainital

Nainital is a picturesque hill station in Uttarakhand. It is near to both Dehradun and Delhi. Nainital has a lovely climate all year that makes it a favorite hill station for families, couples, and even lone travelers. The Nainital's heritage structures, boating, and old temples, as well as stunning views at every turn, attract visitors. Nainital's main attraction is the Naina Lake. Naina Lake has Thandi Sadak on one side, Mall Road on the other, and the bus stop directly across the street. Visitors boat on this picturesque Naini Lake, eat Garhwali and Kumaoni specialties and buy something as souvenirs from here.

5. Amritsar

Ambarsar is a nickname for Amritsar. It is the place where the magnificent Golden Temple is located. This destination is famous for the Jallianwala Bagh massacre and for being close to the Wagah Border. Amritsar market has the best clothes, Amritsari juttis, and accessories that entice the tourist to buy.
